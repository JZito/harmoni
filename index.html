Harmoni is an interactive audio visual experience. With touching the screen, you can play a melody by hand. Without touching the screen, it plays a melody generated by its internal algorithm.

Features

  • High-quality monophonic FM synthsizer.
  • Touch screen virtual keyboard.
  • Algorithmic composition based on fBm (Fractional Brownian Motion).
  • Fractal wave visualizer.
  • Beautiful visual effects.
  • Free and ad-free.
